Title: Responding to an Unresponsive Person
Text: If someone is unresponsive but breathing, first ensure your own safety. Gently tap the person and call their name. Check for normal breathing. If they are breathing, place them in the recovery position and monitor continuously until help arrives.

Title: Checking Breathing
Text: Observe the chest for regular movement. Listen for breathing sounds. If there is no breathing, call for assistance from trained personnel if available and begin only recommended safety procedures.
