Title: Responding to Minor Allergic Reactions
Text: If someone shows mild allergic reaction signs (e.g., itching, rash, swelling), ensure they are in a safe environment. Keep them calm and observe for any changes in condition. Avoid giving any medications.

Title: Monitoring and Comfort
Text: Encourage slow, calm breathing. Keep the person seated or lying comfortably. Remove potential irritants from the area. Focus on safety, reassurance, and monitoring until the person stabilizes.
