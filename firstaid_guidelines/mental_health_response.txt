Title: Responding to Anxiety or Panic
Text: Encourage the person to breathe slowly and focus on one thing at a time. Offer reassurance without pressuring them. Maintain a calm and non-threatening posture.

Title: Responding to Psychological Distress
Text: Do not attempt to diagnose or solve their problem. Listen attentively, validate their emotions, and offer simple supportive guidance. Keep the environment quiet and safe.
